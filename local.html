Location

Workshop events will take place in Berkman Hall 332 on the Cleveland State University campus. Berkman Hall is located at 1899 East 22nd St, Cleveland, OH. Pedestrian access is available at the corner of East 22nd St and Chester Ave, and at the opposite side of the building on Euclide Ave. Download a campus map here.

The closest restrooms are located on the 4th floor of Berkman Hall (find a list of gender neutral restrooms on campus).
Accommodations
The designated hotel for the workshop is the Crowne Plaza Cleveland at Playhouse Square (located at 1260 Euclid Avenue, Cleveland, OH). They are offering us a preferred rate, available by via this link for reservations. Rooms and rates are subject to availability, so we encourage you to make a reseravtion soon. In order to lower lodging costs, we strongly encourage participants to share rooms (please coordinate with your roommate in order to avoid doubling reservations). Once we receive more information about funding, we will be happy to help participants find a roommate.
Transportation

The Crowne Plaza is a short walk from Berkman Hall (10-15 minutes along Euclid Avenue). In alternative, the free E line trolley stops in front of the hotel and close to Berkman Hall.

The Red Line train connects the Cleveland Downtown to Hopkins Internations Airport. For more information on local transportation, visit www.riderta.com.

If you are driving to the workshop, you can download a campus map showing available parking garages close to Berkman Hall.
