Venue

Download a Cleveland State University campus map.

Workshop events will take place in Berkman Hall 332. Berkman Hall is located at 1899 East 22nd St, Cleveland, OH. Pedestrian access is available at the corner of East 22nd St and Chester Ave, and at the opposite side of the building on Euclide Ave.

The closest restrooms are located on the 4th floor of Berkman Hall (find a list of gender neutral restrooms on campus).
