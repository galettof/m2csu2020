Macaulay2 Workshop at Cleveland State University

We are organizing a Macaulay2 workshop to be held at Cleveland State University in Cleveland, OH, May 11-15, 2020. The goal of this workshop is to bring together researchers of different backgrounds and expertise levels to collaborate on developing packages for Macaulay2. Find out more!

This workshop is organized by Federico Galetto, Courtney Gibbons, Hiram López, and Branden Stone.
